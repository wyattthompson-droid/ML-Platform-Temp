Welcome to ML Platform

We build and operate the shared infrastructure that powers machine learning and AI across Datavant. Whether you're training models, running inference, or processing documents at scale, we're here to help you move faster.

Our Mission

Make it easy, fast, and cost-effective for any team at Datavant to build, deploy, and operate ML and AI workloads in production. We provide shared services so teams don't have to reinvent infrastructure — they can focus on solving business problems.

What We Offer

🤖

LLM Serving

Access Claude, GPT, Gemini, and open-source models through Databricks or AWS Bedrock. Built-in cost tracking, rate limiting, and endpoint isolation.

Who Is This For?

Engineers

Need to integrate an LLM into your application? Want to add OCR to your pipeline? Start with our documentation to find the right service, or reach out and we'll help you get set up.

Data Scientists

Looking to train, evaluate, or deploy a model? We provide the Databricks infrastructure, deployment pipelines, and production serving layer so you can focus on the science.

Product Managers

Exploring how ML or AI could solve a business problem? We can help you understand what's feasible, what infrastructure already exists, and how to get from prototype to production.

Leadership & Compliance

Need visibility into ML costs, model governance, or AI usage across the org? Our KPI dashboard tracks production deployments, costs, and platform health in real time.
