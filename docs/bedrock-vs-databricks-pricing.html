Bedrock vs Databricks Pricing

Status: Pending migration from Google Docs

TLDR: Databricks costs ~4% more than Bedrock for Claude models — essentially pricing parity. The 4% reflects a discount we receive from AWS/Bedrock that Databricks doesn't match. Given the scaffolding benefits Databricks provides (tooling, governance, experiment tracking), the recommendation is Databricks unless there's a specific Bedrock-only requirement.

Full pricing comparison tables and model-by-model breakdown to be migrated from Google Docs.

Key finding: OSS models on Databricks may run on our own compute, making the pricing model different (not per-token).
